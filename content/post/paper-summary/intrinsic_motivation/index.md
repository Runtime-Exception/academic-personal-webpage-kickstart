### Summary
In [this](http://www.pyoudeyer.com/ims.pdf) paper, the author provides one mechanism of Intrinsic Motivation which evaluates the progress of learning by comparing similar situations. Situation categorization, dividing situations in different regions, is one unique contribution of the paper.
